CTSO

Throughout the West-Mec welding program studnets will have opportunities to participate in SkillsUSA competiions and learn how to work with other, while also competing at state and at national levels. Students will also complete a team project where they will be presented with a real world scenario and have to work together to create a fabrication using all the skills they learn throughout the program.

To learn more about the SkillsUSA organization you can visit their website by clicking the button below.
