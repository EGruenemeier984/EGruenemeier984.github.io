Welcome

I am an American citizen and have always lived in the United States, but only recently became part of the voting population. I have always found it interesting that for the first seventeen years of most Americans lives, they do not have much of a say into who we elect to represent us in our government. Now that I am eighteen and the presidential election took place, I was exposed to a world that I had not fully understood. With all the presidential election stuff taking place and seeing it in the media, I learned quickly about the electoral college. While seeing all these different article and news headlines I found that there was a lot of people who criticized the electoral college. This led me to my question, Is the electoral college still relevant in today's America?
