What I Know/Want to Find Out

I do not have very much if any knowledge about the electoral college system besides what I learned during the presidential election and some basic knowledge from elementary school. My understanding of the electoral college is that each state has a certain number of votes associated with it and whichever candidate receives the majority of the votes in that state wins that number of electoral votes. This question can be very opinionated, so I want to make sure I take multiple different point of views into consideration when searching for sources. I want to conduct research centering on why the electoral college is used in our presidential election along with finding out any benefits in using the electoral college. In contrast I also want to find any negatives to using the electoral college. I believe this will help successfully find a conclusion to my question.
